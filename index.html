Bianca Niemann - Who am I?

I am a person who at the age of 40 decided to start a new career path and found out I really enjoyed Frontend Development, shortly after immigrating from South Africa to Germany with my husband and our two children.

Learning HTML, CSS and Javascript has been both exciting and challenging and I cannot wait to add more to that list, after so many years of running my own business doing payroll and bookkeeping it is great to see that you are never too old to learn.

I love how no matter how much I learn there is always more information out there, new and different ways to try something, new challenges and new horizons

Working in a team environment is something I am very comfortable with and enjoy a lot, but I am also very capable of working alone and remote as I am very dedicated and able to meet deadlines timeously.
